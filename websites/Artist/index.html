Legion SF presents Chance of Rain for the month of March 2019. Acting as emotional forecasters, artists Leah Rosenberg and Carissa Potter will weather the storm by making soft gestures of self care. Humans have been working on systems of understanding and predicting the future for, well, a while. Leah and Carissa will work on harvesting data from their own emotional ups and own to predict and possibly change the outcome of the future.
